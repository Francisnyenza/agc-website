About AGC

Integrity in professionalism with respect — the values behind a nationally ranked CPA review centre and trusted consultancy.

Who We Are

Associate Group of Accountants (AGC) is a Dar es Salaam-based centre for professional accountancy training and consultancy. We prepare students for the NBAA CPA(T) examinations at all three levels — Foundation, Intermediate and Final — and provide auditing, taxation, finance and accounting consultation services to businesses and individuals.
