Your CPA Pathway

The NBAA CPA(T) qualification has three levels. We teach all of them — start where you are and finish certified.

Step 1

Foundation Level

  • Business Mathematics (A1)
  • Business and Management (A2)
  • Financial Accounting (A3)
  • Cost Accounting (A4)
  • Business Law (A5)
  • Business Economics (A6)
Step 2

Intermediate Level

  • Financial Management (B1)
  • Financial Reporting (B2)
  • Auditing Principles and Practice (B3)
  • Public Finance and Taxation (B4)
  • Performance Management (B5)
  • Government, Ethics and Management
Step 3

Final Level

  • Corporate Reporting (C1)
  • Assurance (C2)
  • Business and Corporate Finance (C3)
  • Advanced Taxation (C4)
